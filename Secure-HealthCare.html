Description

The proposed system consists of two modules i.e., Online Doctor Consultation and Lab Reports Integration. It provides significant help of scheduling online appointment while reducing the waiting time of patients. By using the proposed system, the patients can choose their desired time to consult with a doctor and book the slot from the system. Thus, for the patients that looking for follow up consultation, they can book the consultation time for a future time. Those patients who need faster consultation from doctor like having fever can check for nearest doctors available time and book that slot and reach there on time to avoid long waiting time.

It also automates the management of the diagnostic center making it more efficient, most secured and error free. It aims at standardizing data, consolidating data, ensuring data integrity and reducing inconsistencies. It minimizes the human effort in the basic diagnosis procedure like patient’s information, test schedule, test information etc.


The research objectives provided by this system are: -
  • With real-time blockchain validations, the major goal is to prevent the falsification of prescriptions and lab results, which can result in a variety of crimes.
  • To provide a patient the service to connect instantly with a 24x7 specialist or choose to video visit a particular doctor.
  • Start an instant consultation within 2 minutes or do a video consultation at the scheduled time.
  • To facilitate lab bookings for patients with delivery of reports integrated.
  • To ensure healthcare accessibility to consumers, which is simple, transparent, and available anytime/anywhere when they need it the most.
  • To reduce the turnaround time (TAT) for all the healthcare services.
  • To provide platform that will deliver services that are cost-effective and meet certain pre-established standards of quality.

The application being designed works for the benefits of the society and provides an interactive interface between the patient and the doctor. This system is based on providing healthcare services web application that enables both healthcare providers and patients access accurate and up-to-date information with less time and effort as well as improved efficiency of the information flow. The main advantage of this application is that doctors will be provided with full history of their patients’ health status and patients will hold their data wherever they go along with their lab reports in an secured decentralized manner. It will also help Medical Doctors to speed up diagnosis and treatment of patients through the advice and interaction with the patient. These services are provided in an efficient, cost effective manner, with the goal of reducing the time and resources currently required for such tasks.
